Welcome to Langelihle Guest Lodge

The Original Langelihle perseveres after 10 years in the heart of Clarendon, Pietermaritzburg, SA. The neighborhood attraction has something for everyone—from Liberty Mall to Pietermaritzburg Botanical Gardens to historic sights well–known Midlands Meander, Midmar Dam. And the not-to-miss Farmers Market on Saturday Mornings is a great place for travelers and locals to engage over drinks, food, and good conversation.
